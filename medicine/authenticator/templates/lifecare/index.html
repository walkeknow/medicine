FAQs

What is purpose of PAAV?    

The purpose is to allow the user to verify the authenticity of a product as per their three level of packaging namely Primary, Secondary and Tertiary given by the guidelines of Govt. of India.

How does it work?    

User wishing to authenticate and verify the medicine batch should enter the batch number in the search bar and submit the request. The system will verify from the database and confirm the authentication of the batch of the medicine that you are looking for.

Is the data in the website genuine?    

Disclaimer - 'The data found / not found in our database does not mean that the batch is not a genuine'.

Can a customer verfy his drug with mobile or laptop?    

Yes, provided the data upto primary level has been uploaded by the manufacturer on the Central portal
